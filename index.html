Event Description

November 11, 2017, at 7:00 p.m. SOLD OUT IN RECORD TIME!

Back by overwhelming demand, John Simonelli is returning to Playhouse Square to do a command performance of his latest show Why Would You Do That? at the intimate Connor Palace! If you missed his last performance here, now is your chance to see this amazing show.

Come see some of your all time favorite jokes, stories, and phrases!

  • What if your first name was Chris, and your last name was Mis?
  • Let's torque it.
  • Bup de bup and a boop de boo
  • Will Mikey ever get the chocolate milk to the men on time?!
  • Mouthty mouth and a buff tee buff
